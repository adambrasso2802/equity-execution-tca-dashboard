# Data Dictionary

- Orders:  `order_id`, `trade_date`, `ticker`, `side`, `parent_qty`, `start_dt`, `end_dt`, `urgency`, benchmark fields, arrival/end prices
- Fills: `fill_id`, `order_id`, `fill_dt`, `fill_qty`, `fill_price`, `venue_type`, `fee_bps`, `notional`, `fee_cost`
- OHLCV: `dt`, `ticker`, `open`, `high`, `low`, `close`, `volume`, `pv`, `key`
